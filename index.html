FAQ

Common questions

Do I need design or tech experience?
Nope. We walk you through everything. The only thing you need is a willingness to make videos and post them consistently.
How much time does this take?
Around 5 to 7 hours a week. Each video usually takes 30 to 60 minutes to make and post. You pick your own hours.
How and when do I get paid?
$20 per video, paid weekly through PayPal. View bonuses are calculated monthly. Active creators usually make between $500 and $2,000 a month.
Do I need a new social media account?
Yes, you'll set up a new account just for Playground content. We'll help you get it going, but the account is yours to keep.
Is this a job or internship?
Neither. It's a paid creator program with no office and no fixed hours. Think of it like freelance but with a guaranteed rate per video.
How many spots are available?
We take a limited number of creators per cohort. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis so the sooner you apply, the better.
